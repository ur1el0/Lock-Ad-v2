About Lock-Ad

About the App

Lock-Ad is a mobile-first walking navigation app that helps you choose between the fastest and safest routes. It uses OpenStreetMap data and provides real-time turn-by-turn directions with safety assessments.

APIs & Services
  • OpenStreetMap — Map tiles and geographic data
  • Nominatim — Location geocoding and search
  • OpenRouteService — Route alternatives and detailed directions
  • OSRM — Fast routing engine (default)
  • Bootstrap — UI framework
  • Leaflet — Interactive mapping library

How to Use

1. Select Fastest or Safer route from the menu.

2. Enter your destination (origin is optional; tap "Use my location" to use your current location).

3. Tap Get Directions to calculate the route.

4. View turn-by-turn directions, step count, and safety overview.

5. Enable live tracking to follow your progress as you walk.
